What is the sine of π?

  1. √3/2
  2. √2/2
  3. 1
  4. 0

Explanation

If we look at a unit circle with a radius of 1 (r = 1). We see that the coordinates of π (radians) are (-1, 0). Sine is structured as y/r and 0/1 = 0, giving our answer.
